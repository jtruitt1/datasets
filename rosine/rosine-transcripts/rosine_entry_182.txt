Rose Murphy came May 5th. She was bornin Ireland. Her Father kept a store whichshe attended - She has been marriedtwice but had no Children - Hasbeen 2 1/2 years in the country - During thattime she has been 20 months in the Hospitalin New York and Philadelphia The balanceof the time she lived in Jersey. 5 months atJoe Daviss  She commenced drinking inNew York, and appears to have grow fondof it - She came here from the Almshouse
