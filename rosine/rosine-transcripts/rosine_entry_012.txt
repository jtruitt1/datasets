Page 3  [underlined] 12th - 13th [/underlined] Ann Horn was born in Germany, lost her Parents when young. lived in Baltimore untill her 17th year, was 2 years in a house of ill fame. came to this city & soon after was taken sick & sent to the alms house where she staid some Months, came from there to the Rosine by invitation of two visitors - She was 4 months in the Rosine & then went to R A Mays Mother where she staid 3 months and then returned to the City
