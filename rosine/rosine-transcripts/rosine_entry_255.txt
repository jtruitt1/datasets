Emma Kithcard came 12/29 1856 with IsabellaTaylor - She was born in Elfreths Alley - Her Motherdied when she was about 5 years old & shewas placed with a Mrs Laurence who broughther up - She afterwards hired out till shewas married to [William Curtis Powell], a Gas fitter -He was intemperate, and died with maniapotu two years after - Soon after she got todrinking & went downtown to live. Her FatherJacob Kithcard lives in [Christian Street] above 10th.
