Martha Fry came from Baltimore 1/28/[1854]Her Parents have both died within a yearHer Father was a Thompsonian Physicianbut became intemperate, and left nothingfor his children - There were 4 of them and theywere taken care of by Amelia Grahamwho keeps a Boarding House at the cornerof Saratogo and Liberty Streets - Marthawas placed in two families but havingsome faults, she was returned to MissGraham who did not know what todo with her and knowing about [Sally McHenry]being in the Rosine she broughther on here and bound her to stay [underlined] till 18 [/underlined]
