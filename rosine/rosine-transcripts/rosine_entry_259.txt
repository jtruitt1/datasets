Grace Amler came with the others from the Prison5/22 - She is from Bremen Germany - is 18 years oldHer Parents have died since they came to this CountryShe has lived with two persons with whom shehas learned the Tailoring and worked at ituntil 2 months since when she went with[blank] to Cash's office who seducedher, and she says gave her ten dollars. Sheafterwards went to Selfwrights office in Frontabove Chestnut, and to some other Offices -She thus entered into a life of crime, andgave up work -
