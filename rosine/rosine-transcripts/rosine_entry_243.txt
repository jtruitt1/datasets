Rosanna Swan or McCluskey came April 14 1856 Her Fatherdied in Scotland when she was 6 years. Her Motherlives in Glasgow named Mary Swan. She marriedyoung, had two children that died & herhusband turned out so badly that she cameover her to get away from him. Since shecame to America she has lived at service, butgot to drinking lost her clothing and wassent by Mr Bringhurst to the Rosine -
