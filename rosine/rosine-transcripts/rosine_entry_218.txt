Rosanna Calloway came June 2nd 1854 -She lost her Mother when she was ten yearsold, and lived with an Uncle and camewith his family to this country - They livedin Albany, and were people who indulgedin drink but did not get drunk - Butas the temptation was frequently before hershe became fond of it and if she couldnot get it in the house she would sendout for it - This her friends disapprovedand difficulties ensued which ended inher leaving them
