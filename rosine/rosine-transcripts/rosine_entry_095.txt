112 Eliza Ring came [December] 28th from the Prison with M Townsend - Her Father worked in a Paper Mill and from a mere child she worked in the Mill also - When about 20 she married, but she had lived about 6 months in a bad house, from which her Father took her, and she then worked in the globe Mill till her Husband, a Shomaker put her to board - With him she lived 9 years They both drank, and he had the dropsy & died in the Bucks [County] Almshouse - After his death she came to the city, got into bad company, drank and finally got into prison - She has been there 3 times
