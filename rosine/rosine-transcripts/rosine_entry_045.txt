41   Mary Smith alias Matilda, daughter of  Michael Smith, was born in Jersey City,  is in her 18th year, was brought up an Episcopalian  has been two years in Philadelphia; her father  left here for [New York], giving her a small sum  of money which was not sufficient to maintain  her but a very short time, when it  was expended, she went to live at service  in the family of a Mr Gordon in Marshall  [Street], while there was seduced, and turned out  upon the town about 3 months before coming  to the Rosine House, where she was received  on the 29th of September, 1848.
