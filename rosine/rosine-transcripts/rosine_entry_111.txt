138   Elizabeth Ghonson born in [Philadelphia] is 30  years of age she is widow of Elijah  Ghonson who deserted her and lived  with another woman he is since dead  also all her children she has been  an intemperate woman has been in  the Magdalen also about 6 months  in the Almshouse, came to the Rosine  on Saturday 13th of April signed  the pledges and promises to be  faithful.
