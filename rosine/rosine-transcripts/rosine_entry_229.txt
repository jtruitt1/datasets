Catharine Hanks was received in theRosine May 5th born in this City hasa Mother Sisters & Brothers She says theyare very respectable some of them have notspoken to her for several years on accountof her leading a bad life she has beenliving out at Service lost her placethe people going to the Country She wasthen thrown upon the world withouta home she was tempted could notresist was recomended to come hereshe seems to be in great distress of mindbut a strong desire to lead a differentlife
