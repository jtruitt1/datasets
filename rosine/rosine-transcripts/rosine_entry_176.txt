Jane Barry came July 7th. Her Mother died whenshe was eight years old. Her Father, [William Barry]is a Dyer, but she does not know who he workswith. She was placed with a Mrs Miller whokeeps a Boarding House at Mount Airy.Considering herself ill treated she ran away & cameto the City, and asked permission of a poor Womanto stay with her till she got a place. Seeing anadvertisement in the paper she went to a MrsGregor, 216 Market Street, where she has been for thepast two weeks. She says something is thematter with her, but she is a virtuous girl -
