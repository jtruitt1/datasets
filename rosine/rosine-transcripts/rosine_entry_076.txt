   78Matilda Palmer, is the daughter of Melaine Fayolle, her father died when she was 13 years old her mother still resides in Charleston and has charge of the only child she has ever had to live, she was married at 15 to David S Palmer, who is at present an actor, she was induced to err by Miriam Smith who enticed her to her house and upon her representations she left husband and mother & took her child with her but at the expiration of a month she gave it up to her mother who has kept it ever since. She lived at a house of ill fame in Charleston a few weeks, and then came on to the north where she has lived in different houses of ill fame, in Providence, [New York] and Boston. While ill in [New York] the physician who attended her became interested and sent her from the hospital in [New York] to the Rosine.
