Mary Jane Myers came November 1855.Her Father is William Everly, corner of [Jefferson Avenue],& [Prime Street], a Painter - Her Mother diedwhen she was so young she cannot rememberher - Is now married - She worked awhilein the button factory of her Uncle MillerEverly in Harmony Court 4th near German.Here she got into bad company, was seduced,had a child in the Almshouse, and got to drinking -She went to learn the pantaloon making,but the Woman drank and theyboth drank together but after a while sheresolved to come to the Rosine & has been here22 months
