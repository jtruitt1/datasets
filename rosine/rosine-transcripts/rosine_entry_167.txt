Mary Wallace was brought from Baltimore 12/8/1852She is 15 years old and says she is innocent ofimproper conduct with Men, but had become acquaintedwith some had girls who wished her to do wrong, andthat her Mother at [Doctor] Hartmans instigation senther here - Her Father Samuel Wallace lives in PearlStreet, 4 doors from Saratoga [Street] at Mrs Thomas's -She served 6 months at a Milliners but had goneto school the most of her life
