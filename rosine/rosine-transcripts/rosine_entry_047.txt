44   Mary Taggart admitted September 1st 32 years  of age - Episcopalian - 22 years from Ireland  never married At the age of 24 was seduced  under promise of marriage by a young Man who  had visited her two years but discited her in  3 months and married another - She became  discouraged, thought every body that looked at  her knew what had happened and soon went  to a house to live where she acquired the love  of drink - She has been in the Almshouse twice  in the Magdalen once, in the prison 4 times.
