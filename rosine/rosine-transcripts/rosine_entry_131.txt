Sarah Gillet came 11/1 Born in England but cameover when about 4 years old - Her Father is a Carterand lives in Parker [Street] near Passayunk Road &Fitswater - She has been hiring out from a child -A year a half since she associated with a Womanwho enticed her to drink, and after that she becameconnected with a Man, and lived with him two months -He then deserted her, and she again lived at service -Her Father being sick she left to nurse him, andthen went to live at another place, but they disagreedand she left, and took to drink, and got intoprison, and from prison came to Rosine -She is Sister to Kate Allison
