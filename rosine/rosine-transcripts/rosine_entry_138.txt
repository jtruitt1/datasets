Mary Husten or Eliza Castlore came [December] 31, 1851Her parents died when she was about 5 years oldand she was bound to Cornits Lademus at HardScrabble [Hardscrabble] Delaware where she staid she was 12when she came to [Philadelphia] and went to serviceShe continued at service until she was 17when a Man who pretended to court hertook her to a house of ill fame and left herShe has been living in bad habits ever since,about 8 years -
