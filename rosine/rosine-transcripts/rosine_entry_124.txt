Eliza Williams or Elizabeth Thomas came to Rosine9/22 1851 - Her parents died when she wasabout 9 years old - She was bound by the Guardiansto John Roberts Germantown and staid withthem till she was 15 years old when she left andhired herself out in the City and soon got intocompany and a girl took her to Susan Davis'sin [Plum Street] - She has been boarding from onehouse to another there for two years - She isnow 17 years old -
