Mary Ann White was the Daughter of George Feeblewho lived at the corner of 4th & Brown - She was respectablymarried twice, but was left poor, & had to hire out -She was taken by a Girl who lived in the samehouse to a house of bad character and by degreesgot to drinking and finally lived with Jane SheldonShe has had places, has been in the Rosine three times,but the cursed liquor has obtained such a masteryover that she has not yet been able to persist indoing well more than a few months - She hascome here for the 4th time 3/9/1858
