136   Josephine Wilson or McGinnes Daughter of John Wilson  Lancaster County Born August 10th 1826  married to John McGinnes had one child  husband and child both dead was at service  in Behlers Hotel Harrisburg in 1848 where  during the winter she became acquainted  with Sylvester Cridland who passed as  a single man and brought her to the  City with him under promise of marrying  her, but took her to [Number] 4 [South] 8th to mr  Ransoms and kept her there as his mistress  for about three months when she found out that he was married and left the  house since which time she has been  living on the town
