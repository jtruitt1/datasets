Emma Lebotha or [Emma Shultz] came 11/10 - Her Motherand Step Father [William Shultz] live at the corner of Nectarineand 11th [Streets], next to a Carpenter Shop - He is aBoot fitter - Her Mother is a Tailoress, and workedfor the Stores, & when at home she helped her, but she wouldget restless sometimes, and go out to service for a while -When only 15 she was seduced by a Young Man, andsince then, though she never went to bad houses, yetshe had connection with several different youngMen - She was living at 2nd & Spruce [Streets] at a boardinghouse, and feeling dissatisfied she told her Motherwho persuaded her to come to the Rosine -
