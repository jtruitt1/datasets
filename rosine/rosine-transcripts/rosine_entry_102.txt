122   Josephine Smith real name Larice Ann Groves  daughter of Ann Groves who died at her Daughter  Sarah E Butcher [number] 2 Turner [Street] Catharine & 4th  came to the Rosine [February] 9th 1850 - She had been  bound out, had been 3 years in the House of Refuge,  had afterwards lived with her Mother, worked  at Tailoring in the City, worked in a factory at  Gloster, & finally on an affront with her Sister  came to the city, and went to Mrs Edwards St  Stephens place - From there she has wandered  round to several other houses, and came last  from Mrs Andersons, [number] 3 Osborn court - Margaret Miller brought her here with Mrs Yards permission
