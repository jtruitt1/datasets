Elizabeth Evans 9/28 1853 She is from [Chester County]Her Mother died when she was 3 years old & her Fatherwhen she was six - She lived with her Aunt tillshe was 18, and then hired out. Her Aunt is namedMaria Jones Her Uncl James Jones in East WhitelandShe is enciente, and therefore can not be permitted toremain in the Rosine - As she is not willing to tellher story there is none to report -
