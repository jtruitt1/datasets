- Catherine Foster came to to the Rosine4th month 7th 1855, a resident of [Philadelphia], has 2 children,one married in New York John Foster plasterer.David - in Washington City, Boot Maker.Her husband John Foster has not lived withher for 12 years. Intemperance the cause.Has been intemperate for eight years is willingto take the pledge, and endeavour to doall that is required of her.
