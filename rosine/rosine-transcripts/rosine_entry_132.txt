Bridget McCulloch came 10/20 She came from Irelandwhen about 14 years old - She was sent for by a sister whohad come over before - She lived at service in differentfamilies until 3 years since when she married a Sailorbut he soon went to sea but never returned - Shewent to the Almshouse to be confined, and left the childWhen out of service she staid with a Mrs Murphyand they drank together - She was taken up fordrinking and put in prison where she heard ofRosine and came here from there -
